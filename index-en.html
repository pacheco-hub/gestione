what is Gestione Clienti?

Complete control, effortless management

Gestine Clienti supports you in every aspect of your business, from customer and appointment management to resource control. With the right tools, you work more efficiently and productively.

In Gestione Clienti, you can easily organise your employees' shifts. With centralised management, you can plan schedules, monitor attendance and optimise the distribution of resources, ensuring an efficient workflow at all times.

With Gestione Clienti, your customers can book appointments directly online. Simple and fast, the system allows you to manage bookings in real time, reducing the risk of errors and improving the customer experience.

Gestione Clienti also helps you to keep your warehouse under control. Monitor stock, manage purchases and plan replenishment easily and accurately, avoiding shortages and optimising product availability.

Update your customers with ease. With Gestione Clienti you keep them informed about the status of their subscriptions and send customised notifications as appointment reminders, ensuring a more efficient service and satisfied customers.

With Gestione Clienti, your customers receive a complete report of the treatments performed, including the machines and products used. In this way, you not only provide more transparency, but also offer more security, allowing your customers to feel safe and informed about their health care.

With Gestione Clienti, you not only simplify day-to-day management, you also protect your business from common fines that affect many companies. Our software helps you comply with all data privacy and equipment liability regulations, preventing fines and legal problems that could jeopardise your business. Avoid costly risks and protect your business with a secure and compliant system.

FOREWORD

Pursuant to European Regulation 679/2016 known as the ‘GDPR’, this Privacy Policy describes how Andilab S.r.l. (‘Andilab’ or the ‘Manager’) manages its online platform called ‘Gestione Clienti’ accessible electronically at the address https://gestioneclienti.cloud (hereinafter also the ‘Platform’) in relation to the processing of personal data relating to users accessing it. This information relates exclusively to the Platform accessible via the address https://gestioneclienti.cloud and does not refer to other websites consulted by the user via hypertext links that may be present on the aforementioned Platform. The use of other websites and services, even through links present on the Platform, may be subject to different terms and privacy policies.

ROLE OF TREATMENT

As a result of using the Platform, data relating to identified or identifiable natural persons may be processed. Andilab S.r.l., a company with registered office in Via dei Normanni n. 17, 65014 Loreto Aprutino (PE), P.IVA/C.F. 02326560683, info@andilab.it, acts as the Data Controller (hereinafter ‘Controller’) for the personal data of users collected when (i) accessing; (ii) registering; (iii) using the Platform and/or (iv) requesting assistance and customer care. In the event of the insertion of personal data of other parties by authorised users (e.g. patients' personal data), the User is also the Data Controller, limited to the data entered for which it has obtained appropriate consent from the data subjects (and, in particular, the rights provided for in Articles 15-21 of the GDPR).

CONTACT DETAILS

Users may contact Andilab for all questions relating to the processing of their personal data and/or in connection with the management of data processed through the ‘Customer Management’ platform, as well as for the exercise of their rights under the Regulation at the following address: dpo@andilab.it.

TYPES OF DATA PROCESSED

All data collected and processed are managed in compliance with current EU and national legislation, including laws, regulations issued by Authorities legitimated to do so. As Data Controller, Andilab will process (i) technical operating data not sent to third parties (unique IDs and data from the computer device used) when browsing the Platform; (ii) personal data (access credentials) provided by the user for the purposes of accreditation to Customer Management; (iii) data entered by authorised users through the Platform and (iv) any information provided by the user requesting information/assistance.

COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING SYSTEMS

Technical and anonymised analytical cookies are used, no user profiling cookies are used. For more detailed information on cookies and their retention times please see our Cookie Policy at the following link Cookie Policy.

TREATMENT AND LEGAL BASES

Andilab will process personal data in order to: (i) allow viewing and access to the Platform; (ii) allow registration to the Platform and (iii) the use of services reserved for registered users; (iv) execute requests sent by the user. The operating information collected directly (browsing) and that sent to third parties (i) is collected automatically in an anonymous and aggregate form both to detect any technical problems and for statistical purposes related to the use of the Platform, to improve the user-friendliness and access to the functionalities provided. The processing of such data does not require the consent of the data subject (Art. 6(1)(f) of the GDPR). The provision of personal data for (iv) any requests for information and/or assistance is not mandatory, and is attributable to Article 6(1)(b), last paragraph of the GDPR or, as the case may be, the legitimate interest of Andilab (Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR) and is strictly instrumental to the fulfilment of the request.

PROCESSING METHODS AND STORAGE TIMES

Processing is carried out electronically, with logic strictly related to the aforementioned purposes, kept only for the time necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, and will be deleted at the end of this period, unless the data needs to be retained for legal obligations or to enforce a legal claim. For example, we may need to retain your personal data to ensure the quality of the service provided, or we may need to retain it to defend ourselves against future legal claims. For (i) data related to site navigation, the deletion policies provide for immediate deletion after their aggregation, while data provided by you for (ii) registration and (iii) use of the Platform will be retained for ten years after their receipt. The (iv) data relating to requests for information and/or assistance will be retained by Andilab for one year from the submission of the request. Andilab informs you that specific security measures are observed to prevent the loss of data, unlawful or incorrect use and unauthorised access.

RECIPIENTS OF DATA

The data may, for the pursuit of the purposes previously described, be communicated and known to/by: employees and subordinate workers and, in general, to Andilab staff, who will operate as Authorized Persons for the processing of personal data, specifically appointed by the Data Controller. The user's data may also be processed by Aruba S.p.A. provider of services for the management of IT systems and maintenance of the IT infrastructure, this entity will operate as Data Controller specifically appointed by the Data Controller pursuant to art. 28 of the GDPR The processing and storage of data are carried out in Italy and no transfers to other non-EU countries are envisaged, nor will such data be disclosed to the public. Andilab ensures that in the event of transfer of the processed data, the mechanisms to protect the interested party referred to in the articles will be adopted. 44 et seq. of the GDPR.

RIGHTS OF THE INTERESTED PARTIES

The user of the ‘Customer Management’ Platform may exercise his or her rights under the GDPR by contacting the Controller via the email address dpo@andilab.it. In particular, he/she will be able to request access to personal data concerning him/her, rectification or deletion or he/she will be able to request restriction of processing and/or object to processing. In addition, you will have the right to data portability and should you wish to lodge a complaint, you may also lodge it with the Data Protection Authority.
